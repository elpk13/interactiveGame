
   About this game:

   Wolf Adventure was made in 2021.

   This educational game is hosted on a Raspberry Pi and 
   is written in Python utilizing the Pygame set of 
   modules. 

   There are six characters to choose from (one based on 
   each of Wolf Park's current wolves) and three chapters
   that allow players to choose which part of the wolf's 
   life they would play during. The game can also be 
   played sequentially. 

   This text can be updated (to include a complete list of
   image credits, etc.) by editing the text file "about.txt".

   The properties of this text can be edited in "about.py".

   Press spacebar to exit.
